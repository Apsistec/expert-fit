About App Designed and Developed by Obliquity

The X Fitness Studio App is a progressive web app (PWA) created for fitness and health enthusiast who are interested at reaching and maintaining higher levels of success within their training regimine. As a PWA, the X Fitness Studio App is capable of working on most browsers including Safari, Chrome, Chrome based browsers, Edge, Samsung, and others. In addition, the PWA is capable of working on most devices including Apple, Linux, and Windows, which includes most operating systems (OS) such as iOS, Android, Windows, and smart TV OSs.
